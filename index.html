About me

Combining my passions for yoga and software development allows me to find creative solutions and explore innovative ways to reach people both online and offline.

About this website

This website has its origins in the context of my coding course. It serves as a platform where I unite my deep passion for yoga with my newfound creative expression in the world of software development. It's an exciting fusion of two seemingly disparate worlds, where I channel my inner yogi and tech enthusiast to create a space where I can share my experiences, insights, and projects.
